Amgydala notes

Psychiatric care occupies a complex and vast space in society. Mental health ailments affect billions. For those who suffer, drug-based solutions represent the standard method of therapy, which may prove more hurtful than helpful, in the short- and long-term.

Conversations about best practices for anti-psychotic medicine do not happen easily. Byzantine and sometimes contradictory narratives obscure the realities of the drug industry, its participants, and the drugs themselves. And yet, these conversations matter deeply.

“Amygdala Notes” provides a space for contemplating the risks of drug treatment in mental health care. This series aims to build awareness about problematic prescription practices in psychiatric care by using music as a point of entry for thinking about difficult political issues.
